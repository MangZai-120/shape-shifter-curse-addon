## About This Mod

The **Shape Shifter Curse Addon** is an add-on for [Shape Shifter Curse](https://www.mcmod.cn/class/20760.html), built on Fabric. It adds brand-new **SP (Special) Form** variants on top of the vanilla forms — each with reworked active skills, passive talents, and a unique combat style.

## Core Gameplay

> "The essence of the curse is not destruction, but evolution... When the moonlight is swallowed by deeper shadow, only Shape Shifters who dare to embrace extreme power can reach those forbidden forms."

Tired of plain animal instincts? Time to break through!

**Keys of Evolution**: Use the [Moon Essence Cross](items/evolution_items.md#moon-essence-cross) or the [Evolution Stone](items/evolution_items.md#evolution-stone) under the right conditions to evolve your current form into an advanced SP form, and feel the reshaping and fusion of magic.

- Every SP Form has a fully reworked skin, active kit, and passive talents
