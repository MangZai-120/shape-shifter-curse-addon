## File No.: FF-E-013

### 80th Familiar Evolution Test

**Status: Project Terminated — Top Secret**

---

**Subject**: 80th Familiar Evolution Test

**Lead Researcher**: \<Classified\>

### Ⅰ. Experimental Summary

This experiment was intended to study whether newly-created beings could undergo further modification.

### Ⅱ. Materials

1. **Subject #13**: A human exhibiting moderate red-fox-form mutation
2. **Moondust Pure Crystal**: To study whether further curse modification was possible
3. **Experimental Catalyst**: To study whether the curse progression could be accelerated
4. **Linking Collar — Test Model**: To study whether normal control was possible

### Ⅲ. Environment

Purple Moon: variable uncontrolled, but a required condition.

### Ⅳ. Procedure

1. Restrain the subject at the center of the moonlight well
2. Force the subject to consume the Experimental Catalyst
3. After transformation, fit the subject with the Linking Collar
4. Have the subject consume the Moondust Pure Crystal

### Ⅴ. Results

| Stage | Result |
|-------|--------|
| Stage 1 | After consuming the catalyst, the subject exhibited intense pain. The body's transformation accelerated beyond normal speed, with visible changes to the naked eye and intensified spiritual fluctuations around it |
| Stage 2 | The subject began to convulse, body fully transformed into a red fox form but with two tails. Low-temperature blue flames began to emanate around it |
| Stage 3 | Aggression toward Marauders and the Witch was nearly zero, but it displayed strong hostility toward villagers |
| Stage 4 | After fitting the collar, the subject came fully under the Witch's control, obeying even orders to harm itself |
| Stage 5 | After consuming the diluted Moondust Pure Crystal, the subject lost control. Its body enlarged, and the blue flames it emitted caused both burns and frostbite simultaneously (minimum temperature recorded: -10°C). After roughly half a minute of rampage, it collapsed and died. Dissection revealed a ruptured energy-storage organ, with internal organs both scorched and frozen |

### Ⅶ. Analysis

Similar to previous results, the subject died after consuming the Moondust Pure Crystal. Extensive experimentation indicates that **Familiar Red Foxes cannot undergo further deep modification**, having reached the upper limit of curse tolerance.

### Ⅷ. Recommendation

Cease further development of Familiar enhancement research. The damage caused by each experiment has cost the Marauders multiple key researchers. Even if one could successfully cause a Familiar to consume the Moondust Pure Crystal and evolve, there is an overwhelming probability of producing an uncontrollable berserk beast.

## File No.: MR-A-007

### Moon Essence Cross Energy Channeling and Form Breakthrough Test

**Status: Sealed Research**

---

**Subject**: Moon Essence Cross Energy Channeling and Form Breakthrough Test

**Lead Researcher**: \<Classified\>

### Ⅰ. Experimental Summary

This experiment was intended to investigate the activation conditions of the ancient relic the "Moon Essence Cross" under specific environments, and its capacity to intervene in the form of a cursed host.

### Ⅱ. Materials

1. **Subject #73**: A human exhibiting permanent wolf-form mutation
2. **Moon Essence Cross**: An intact sample recovered from the ruins
3. Vital Signs Monitor Mk-II
4. High-density Restraint Field

### Ⅲ. Environment

Artificially simulated Purple Moon environment (85% fidelity); in the late stages, real Purple Moon illumination was introduced.

### Ⅳ. Procedure

1. Perform a basic physical examination of the subject and record curse erosion level
2. Place the Moon Essence Cross around the subject's neck
3. Gradually increase the intensity of simulated Purple Moon illumination, introducing real moonlight at the critical point
4. Observe the subject's form changes and vital signs

### Ⅴ. Results

| Stage | Result |
|-------|--------|
| Stage 1 | Under simulated illumination alone, the Moon Essence Cross showed no obvious reaction, with only faint glow along the surface patterns. The subject reported a sensation of "a cold gaze" |
| Stage 2 | Upon introduction of real Purple Moon light, the Moon Essence Cross burst into silver radiance instantly, forcibly piercing the subject's skin and fusing with the flesh |
| Stage 3 | The subject screamed; the original werewolf form began to collapse, bones shattering and reforming. Life force drained at an astonishing speed, being converted into high-density spiritual energy |
| Stage 4 | When life force reached the critical point, the form transformed qualitatively, body enlarging to 1.5×, fur taking on a crystalline quality. The shackles of form were broken |
| Stage 5 | The subject lost control and tore through the restraint field. Due to total life-force exhaustion, it suddenly collapsed and turned to ashes within seconds. Only the lusterless Moon Essence Cross remained |

### Ⅶ. Analysis

The Moon Essence Cross does possess the ability to break the shackles of cursed forms, granting the host power far exceeding the norm. However, its energy source is not external free spiritual energy, but **directly drawn from the user's life essence**.

> The ancient record stating *"the walker's life is its kindling"* is not a metaphor, but a **statement of fact**.

Until an alternative energy source or a means to reduce the consumption is found, any act of wearing it is equivalent to suicide.

### Ⅷ. Recommendation

Seal the Moon Essence Cross within the high-risk containment zone. It is recommended that subsequent research direction shift toward using external charging to replace life-force consumption, or seek a special constitution capable of withstanding such consumption. **Any unauthorized contact is forbidden.**
