# Evolution Items

Evolution items are key tools for SP form evolution. Different forms require different evolution items to trigger their evolution.

---

## Moon Essence Cross

**Moon Essence Cross**

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Item command | `/give @p ssc_addon:sp_upgrade_thing 1` |
| Max stack | 1 per stack |
| Category | Evolution Item |

### Description

> "A golden cross condensed from moonlight and curse. When the purple moonlight shrouds the land, only Shape Shifters who have broken free from flesh-and-blood bondage can touch its true power at the moment of lunar conjunction — either transforming into a form beyond mortal flesh, or having the surging frenzy within the Moon Essence tear their soul apart, exploding into a brilliant and lethal stardust rain. This is neither a key nor a blade: it is a twin contract sealed with the moonlight — half a radiance of sublimation, half a frozen rupture of annihilation. Remember — only the moonlight reigns supreme."

### Crafting Recipe

Craft on a crafting table:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Gold Ingot | 4 |
| Emerald | 1 |
| Redstone Dust | 1 |
| Shaping Core | 1 |
| Moondust Pure Crystal | 1 |
| Netherite Ingot | 1 |

!!! note "Layout"
    All materials other than Gold Ingots and the Shaping Core can be arranged freely. Gold Ingots go in the four corners; Shaping Core goes in the center.

### Usage Conditions & Effects

This item is used for evolutions related to the Cursed Moon. The player must meet **both** conditions to use it successfully:

| Condition Combination | Effect |
|-----------------------|--------|
| Neither condition met | No effect, Moon Essence Cross is consumed |
| Only Cursed Moon condition met | No effect, Moon Essence Cross is consumed |
| Only permanent form condition met | No effect, Moon Essence Cross is consumed |
| **Both met** | **Evolve into SP form, Moon Essence Cross is consumed** |
| Already SP form + no Cursed Moon | No effect, Moon Essence Cross is consumed |
| Already SP form + Cursed Moon active | Block-destroying explosion (no damage in Peaceful; on other difficulties deals 100000 damage / lethal) |

!!! warning "Danger"
    The Moon Essence Cross explosion is similar to an End Crystal. It deals **100000 damage** to the player using it. A Totem of Undying can protect against the lethal explosion, so it can serve as an emergency burst-damage option.

### Applicable Forms

- SP Familiar Fox (Spirit Realm Lord)
- SP Axolotl (Abyssal Overlord)
- SP Fallen Allay (Spirit of the Fallen)
- SP Anubis Wolf (Netherjudge)
- Desmodus (Bloodthirster)

## Evolution Stone

**Evolution Stone**

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Item command | `/give @p ssc_addon:evolution_stone 1` |
| Max stack | 1 per stack |
| Category | Evolution Item |

### Description

> "A gemstone seemingly dredged up from the deepest dream, its shape resembling an eternal cat's eye gazing into the abyss. In the quiet of night, you can glimpse the magnificent universe within: sculk-blue veins spread like the nerves of fate, the crimson embers of the hunt slowly pulse at its core, the firefly glow of night vision and the void nebula of the final curtain weave and circle within — and all light is ultimately gently swallowed by the boundless bedrock of shadow, settling into a quiet pitch-black."

> "When five hardship-tempered shadow shards merge in ritual, they are no longer ordinary creations. It has a pulse and breath, like a sleeping heart, whispering ancient songs of stealth, hunting, endurance, vision, and the void into your palm. It is the ultimate crystallization of nocturnal power, waiting only for a resonant host to complete that fated awakening."

### Crafting Recipe

Crafted on a crafting table from the five [Evolution Shards](materials.md):

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Hunt Shard | 1 |
| Sculk Shard | 1 |
| Ender Shard | 1 |
| Night Vision Shard | 1 |
| Shadow Shard | 1 |

### Usage

Hold right-click to use. Used for evolutions **not requiring the Cursed Moon**.

- For a **non-target form** use: failure prompt, nothing happens
- For a **target form** use: success prompt, Evolution Stone is consumed

### Applicable Forms

- Wild Cat (SP) (Moonlight Phantom)
- SP Snow Fox (Frost Spirit)
- SP Allay (Crystal Sound)
- Golden Sandstorm (SP)
- Mancianima (Forbidden Weapon)
- Parasitic Fruit Bat (Moving Orchard)
