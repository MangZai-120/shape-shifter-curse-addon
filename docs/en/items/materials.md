# Materials

This mod adds various materials, mainly used to craft evolution items and other tools.

---

## Evolution Shards

Five scattered shards containing different essences, used to craft the [Evolution Stone](evolution_items.md#evolution-stone).

### Shadow Shard

**Shadow Shard**

> The ink-black surface refracts a metallic luster like Netherite, with an extraordinarily heavy and hard texture — like the toughest bedrock in the earth's depths. It silently absorbs all surrounding light.

### Night Vision Shard

**Night Vision Shard**

> Inside this translucent blue-green shard, golden specks of light slowly swirl like flowing sand. Held in hand, it feels as light as a phantom wing, faintly granting clarity of vision like Night Vision.

### Ender Shard

**Ender Shard**

> Deep within the shard lies swirling starry purple and dragon-breath pale violet clouds, with occasional ender particles flickering past. It seems to resonate with the distant End Void, bringing an uneasy throb.

### Hunt Shard

**Hunt Shard**

> The shard shows hues of dark red interwoven with charcoal black, its edges scorching like lava. Listening closely, it seems to seal the roar of flames and the wail of Ghasts, radiating an endless craving for battle.

### Sculk Shard

**Sculk Shard**

> This deep-purple crystal shard feels cold to the touch, with sculk-like blue veins slowly pulsing inside. As you approach, you hear a faint echo — as if countless eyes are watching you from the shadows.

## Coral Ball

**Coral Ball**

A ball made of Active Coral, related to the SP Axolotl form's abilities.

---

## Modified Vanilla Items

### Totem of Undying

This mod modifies the Totem of Undying. It can be used to negate the death from a Moon Essence Cross explosion, so it doubles as an emergency burst option.

### Beacon

The Beacon has been adjusted to accommodate the new status effects.
