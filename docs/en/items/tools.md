# Tools

This mod adds various utility items.

---

## Water Spear

**Water Spear**

Exclusive weapon of the SP Axolotl form.

### Acquisition

In SP Axolotl form, right-click with an arrow in the off-hand to obtain a Water Spear.

### Effect

- Duration: **1 minute**
- Hits deal damage and apply **Slowness**
- Enemies within a small radius also take **splash damage**

---

## Friend Marker

**Friend Marker**

A tool to mark friendly players, preventing accidental damage from your own skills.

### Usage

Use it on a target player to mark them as a friend. Marked players are not affected by certain of your AOE skills.

---

## Clear Friend Marker

**Clear Friend Marker**

A tool to remove a previously applied friend mark.

### Usage

Use it on a player already marked as a friend to remove their friendly status.

---

## Hotbar Skills

### Healing Spell

**Healing Spell**

A hotbar active skill of the SP Familiar Fox form — consume mana to heal yourself or allies.

### Allay Jukebox

**Allay Jukebox**

A hotbar skill related to the SP Allay form.
