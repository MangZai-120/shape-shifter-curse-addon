# Shape Shifter Curse Addon - Advanced Gameplay Guide
# 幻形者诅咒附属 - 进阶玩法指南

This guide details the advanced content provided by this addon, specifically focusing on the new SP Forms (Special Forms) and the new items associated with them.
本指南详细介绍了此附属模组提供的进阶内容，重点关注新的SP形态（特殊形态）以及与其相关的新增物品。

---

## 1. Evolution Items
## 1. 进化用品

### 1.1 The Moon Marrow Cross Ring
### 1.1 月髓十字环

The **Moon Marrow Cross Ring** is a core artifact introduced in this expansion. It appears to be condensed from pure Moondust Crystal, serving as a key to unlocking deeper levels of the curse.
**月髓十字环**是本扩展包引入的核心道具。它看似由纯净的月尘纯晶凝结而成，是解锁更深层诅咒的关键。

#### How to Obtain
#### 如何获得

The ring is crafted using **Gold Ingots**, **Emeralds**, **Moondust Crystal Shards**, **Netherite Ingots**, and **Redstone**. Please check your recipe book (or REI/JEI) for the exact crafting recipe.
该环需要用金锭，绿宝石，月尘纯晶，下界合金锭，红石制作，具体的合成配方请查看你的配方书（或REI/JEI）。

#### Usage & Effect
#### 用途与效果

Its primary function is to allow the bearer to "absorb more curse". In gameplay terms, holding this ring and being in the final stage of evolution are prerequisites for evolving into **SP Forms**. It stabilizes the otherwise volatile magic required for these supreme transformations.
它的主要功能是允许持有者“吸收更多诅咒”。在游戏中，持有此环和进化为最终形态是进化为**SP形态**的先决条件。它能稳定这些至高变形所需的、原本不稳定的魔法。

### 1.2 Evolution Stone
### 1.2 进化石

A living energy core fused from five shadow shards in a dark night ritual. Sealed within are veins of sculk, embers of the hunt, bedrock of shadow, ribbons of night vision, and the void of the end. It awaits to unleash its true power.
一块由五种暗影碎片在暗夜仪式中融合而成的活性能量核心。内部封印着幽匿的脉络、猎杀的余烬、暗影的基石、夜视的流光与终幕的虚空。它正等待着释放其真正的力量。

#### How to Obtain
#### 如何获得

It is synthesized from five specific types of **Shadow Shards**.
它由五种特定类型的**暗影碎片**合成而来。

#### Usage & Effect
#### 用途与效果

The Evolution Stone allows the Wild Cat form to evolve into SP Wild Cat.
进化石可使野猫形态进化为sp野猫。

### 1.3 Evolution Shards
### 1.3 进化碎片

Scattered fragments containing different essences required to craft the Evolution Stone.
散落的碎片，包含制作进化石所需的不同精华。

- **Shadow Shard**: The ink-black fragment refracts a Netherite-like metallic luster. Its texture is incredibly heavy and hard, like the toughest bedrock deep in the earth. It silently absorbs all surrounding light.
- **暗影碎片**: 墨黑色的碎片表面折射出下界合金般的金属光泽，其质地异常沉重坚硬，宛若大地深处最坚韧的基石。它无声地吸收着周围所有的光线。

- **Night Vision Shard**: Inside this translucent turquoise fragment, golden light dust swirls slowly like quicksand. Holding it feels as light as a Phantom, faintly bringing a clear vision like Night Vision.
- **夜视碎片**: 这块半透明的青绿色碎片内部，有流沙般的金色光尘在缓缓旋转。握在手中能感到幻翼般的轻灵，并隐约带来夜视般的清晰视野。

- **Ender Shard**: Deep within the fragment lies swirling starry purple and dragon-breath-like lilac clouds, with occasional Ender particles flashing by. It seems to resonate with the distant End Void, causing a sense of uneasy palpitation.
- **终幕碎片**: 碎片深处是旋转的星空紫与龙息般的淡紫色云絮，偶尔有末影粒子一闪而过。它似乎与远方的末地虚空产生了某种共鸣，令人感到一丝不安的悸动。

- **Hunt Shard**: The fragment displays a color of interwoven dark red and charcoal black, with edges scorching like lava. Listen carefully, it seems to seal the roar of Blazes and the wail of Ghasts, radiating an endless thirst for battle.
- **猎杀碎片**: 碎片呈现出暗红与炭黑交织的色泽，边缘如熔岩般灼热。仔细聆听，其中似乎封印着烈焰的咆哮与恶魂的哀泣，散发着对战斗的无尽渴望。

- **Sculk Shard**: This deep purple crystal fragment is cold to the touch, with cyan veins pulsing slowly inside like Sculk veins. When you get close, you can hear faint echoes, as if countless eyes are staring at you from the dark.
- **幽匿碎片**: 这块深紫色的晶体碎片触手冰凉，内部仿佛有幽匿脉络般的青色纹路在缓缓搏动。当你靠近时，能听到微弱的回声，仿佛有无数双眼睛在暗处凝视着你。

## 2. Familiar Fox SP: Spirit Messenger
## 2. 使魔红狐 SP：灵界使者

This form represents the ultimate evolution of the Familiar Fox. You are no longer just a magical pet; you have become a master of the spirit realm.
此形态代表了使魔红狐的终极进化。你不再仅仅是一只魔法宠物；你已经成为了灵界的主宰。

### Transformation
### 变形

To achieve this form, you typically need to be in the final stage of the **Familiar Fox** form. Evolution is possible only under the combined influence of the **Cursed Moon** and the **Moon Marrow Cross Ring**.
要达成此形态，你通常需要处于**使魔红狐**形态的最终阶段，并在诅咒之月与月髓十字环的共同作用下才可进化。

### Mana & Abilities
### 魔力与能力

Unlike the standard Familiar Fox, the SP form has a more complex Mana system:
- **Blue Fire Ring**: You can toggle a ring of blue fire around you. It burns nearby enemies but drains mana continuously.
- **Soul Reap**: Killing enemies restores Mana. If your Fire Ring is active, it also recovers extra energy and heals you.
- **Spirit Body**: You are immune to more negative status effects than before.
- **Fox Fire Breath**: A devastating active attack that spews blue flames.
- **Soul Fire**: A special fire attack, same effect as vanilla **Ignite**, but cannot be extinguished by water.

与普通使魔红狐不同，SP形态拥有更复杂的魔力系统：
- **苍蓝火环**：你可以开启环绕周身的苍蓝火环。它会持续灼烧附近的敌人，但会持续消耗魔力。
- **灵魂收割**：击杀敌人会恢复魔力。如果你的火环处于激活状态，它还会额外回复能量并治疗你。
- **灵体化身**：你比之前免疫了更多负面状态。
- **狐火吐息**：一种毁灭性的主动攻击，喷吐出蓝色火焰。
- **灵魂火**：一种特殊的火焰攻击，效果同原版的**点燃**，但无法被水扑灭。

### Risks (Changes from Old Form)
### 风险（与旧形态的变化）

The cost of power is high. Mana is now your lifeline.
- **Mana Starvation**: If Mana drops below 10%, you suffer severe penalties to movement and damage. If it hits 0, it is torture.
- **Mana Explosion**: Hints suggest absorbing too much mana might have explosive consequences.
- **Fragility**: Like before, you cannot wear leg or foot armor, and your physical health remains low.
- **Spirit Body**: The magic within you will also make beneficial potion effects on you vanish.

力量的代价是高昂的。魔力现在是你的生命线。
- **魔力匮乏**：如果魔力低于10%，你的移动和伤害会受到严厉惩罚。如果归零，那将是一种折磨。
- **魔力过载**：提示表明吸收过多的魔力可能会导致爆炸性的后果。
- **脆弱**：和以前一样，你无法穿戴护腿或靴子，且物理生命值依然很低。
- **灵体化身**：你体内的魔力也会让对你有益的药水效果消失。

## 3. Axolotl SP: Deep Sea Lurker
## 3. 美西螈 SP：深海潜伏者

A specialized evolution focusing on survival and water mastery.
专注于生存和水下掌控的特化进化。

### Unique Item: Axolotl Moisturizer
### 专属物品：美西螈加湿器

The **Axolotl Moisturizer** is a lifesaver for this form.
- **How to Obtain**: Crafted using **Iron Ingots**, **Redstone**, and a **Sponge**.
- **Usage**: Right-click while holding to toggle on/off. It maintains your humidity levels when out of water.
- **Recharge**: Craft with a Bucket of Axolotl to refill its energy.
- **Effect**: Continuously restores SP Axolotl's moisture, lasting up to 90 minutes.

**美西螈加湿器**是此形态的救命稻草。
- **如何获得**: 需要铁锭，红石，海绵合成。
- **用法**：手持该物品右键点击切换开关。它能在离开水时维持你的湿润度。
- **充能**：使用美西螈桶合成以补充能量。
- **效果**：持续回复sp美西螈的湿润度，最多持续90分钟。



### Abilities
### 能力

- **Play Dead**: You can actively enter a "Play Dead" state. Monster aggro is lost, accompanied by blindness and 90% damage reduction, while rapidly regenerating health.
- **Rain Wetness**: Direct exposure to rain now recovers moisture, making land travel viable during storms.
- **Enhanced Water Burst**: Your active water attack deals significantly higher damage than the standard form.
- **Vortex Impact**: An active skill that pulls in enemies and deals high damage. During the cast, you are slowed by 50% and gain 30% damage immunity.
- **Water Spear**: Holding an arrow in your off-hand and right-clicking grants a Water Spear lasting 1 minute. Hits deal damage and slowness, causing extra splash damage to nearby enemies.

- **假死**：你可以主动进入“假死”状态。怪物仇恨消失，伴随失明效果和90%的减伤，并迅速恢复生命值。
- **雨润**：直接暴露在雨中现在可以恢复湿润度，使风暴期间的陆地旅行成为可能。
- **强化水流爆破**：你的主动水流攻击造成的伤害远高于标准形态。
- **涡流冲击**：吸附敌人并造成高额伤害的主动技能，释放期间自己减速50%，免疫30%的伤害。
- **水矛**：副手拿箭右键可获得一根持续一分钟的水矛，命中可造成伤害并附带减速效果，小范围内的敌人也会受到额外伤害。

### Risks (Changes from Old Form)
### 风险（与旧形态的变化）

None
无

## 4. Wild Cat SP: Night Stalker
## 4. 野猫 SP：暗夜猎手

'When power of the wild awakens completely, you are no longer just a wild cat... You are the ghost of the jungle, the hunter of the night'
'当野性的力量完全觉醒，你便不再仅仅是一只野猫… 你是丛林的幽灵，暗夜的猎手'

### Unique Item: Cloak of Invisibility
### 专属物品：隐匿斗篷

- **How to Obtain**: Crafted using two **Phantom Membranes** sandwiching an **Ender Eye**.
- **Usage**: Worn in the chestplate slot (or automatically active as accessory? Check in-game, assumed passive or equipable).
- **Effect**: Increases True Invisibility duration by 2s, but increases cooldown by 2s. Exclusive to Wild Cat SP.

- **如何获得**: 使用两个**幻翼膜**中间夹一个**末影之眼**合成。
- **用法**：装备在胸甲槽（或作为饰品生效）。
- **效果**：增加真实急迫持续时间2秒，但增加冷却时间2秒。野猫SP专属。

### Abilities
### 能力

- **True Invisibility**: Press the Primary Ability key to enter True Invisibility. You become invisible to all mobs. Attacking or using items will break invisibility.
- **Stun Dash**: While in True Invisibility, press the Secondary Ability key to dash forward. Upon landing, stuns nearby enemies.
- **Ink Blindness**: Right-click while holding an Ink Sac in the main hand to blind nearby creatures.
- **Night Walker**: Movement speed increased by 10% at night.
- **Power of the Night**: Attack damage increased by 1 at night.
- **Feral Diet**: Hunger and saturation granted by eating raw food are doubled.
- **Wall Climbing**: You can climb walls.
- **Weakness Spotting**: Manually cancelling True Invisibility by pressing the Primary Ability key again grants 5 seconds of Guaranteed Critical Hits and Speed II.

- **真实隐身**：按下主要技能键进入真实隐身状态。所有生物无法看见你。攻击或使用物品会破隐。
- **震慑冲刺**：真实隐身期间按下次要技能键，向前冲刺。落地时震慑周围敌人。
- **墨囊致盲**：主手持有墨囊右键，致盲周围生物。
- **夜行者**：夜晚移动速度增加10%。
- **暗夜之力**：夜晚攻击伤害增加1点。
- **野性食谱**：生食获得的饱食度翻倍。
- **爬墙**：你可以攀爬墙壁。
- **弱点打击**：真实隐身期间再次按下主动技能键时主动解除真实隐身，获得5秒的必定暴击与速度II效果。

### Risks (Changes from Old Form)
### 风险（与旧形态的变化）

- **Daylight Sluggishness**: Movement speed decreased by 10% during day.
- **Armor Restriction**: Cannot wear Leggings or Boots.

- **白昼迟缓**：白天移动速度减少10%。
- **护甲限制**：无法穿戴护腿和靴子。
